Biography

Luan Guilherme Lourenço is a dual-competency professional — graduated simultaneously in Software Engineering and Accounting Sciences at the Catholic University of Brasília. In a market that rarely finds someone mastering both universes with real depth, Luan occupies a strategic and unique position.


His specialty is turning accounting and tax complexity into automated efficiency. With technical command over the main ERP systems in the Brazilian market — TOTVS, Omie, Domínio and Questor — and the ability to develop solutions in Python and API integration, he delivers what companies and accounting firms seek: less rework, fewer errors, and more results.


Based in Brasília, DF, Luan acts as the bridge between the accounting department and the tech team. While other professionals need an intermediary to translate technical requirements into business language — or vice-versa — Luan does both. This ability to move between worlds is what makes him irreplaceable wherever he works. — Software Engineering · Accounting Sciences · Automation · Compliance · Brasília, DF
